Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is Klik?
Klik is a finance app designed to help you manage your money with simplicity and privacy.
How do I get started with Klik?
Download the Klik app from the App Store or Google Play, then follow the on-screen steps to set up and start using the app.
Is Lokal available in my area?
Lokal is continuously expanding to new areas. When you sign up, the app will use your location to show you available services nearby. If there are no services in your area yet, you can join our waitlist and we'll notify you when we launch in your region.

For Customers

How do I book a service?
Browse or search for the service you need, select a provider, choose your preferred date and time, and confirm your booking. You'll receive a notification when the provider accepts your request. You can track your order status and communicate with the provider through the in-app chat.
Can I cancel or reschedule a booking?
Yes! You can cancel or reschedule your booking through the Orders section in the app. Please note that cancellation policies may vary by provider. Check the provider's cancellation policy before booking, and try to cancel at least 24 hours in advance to avoid any fees.
How do I rate and review a service provider?
After your service is completed, you'll receive a notification to rate your experience. You can rate the provider from 1-5 stars and leave a written review. Your feedback helps other customers make informed decisions and helps providers improve their services.
What if I'm not satisfied with a service?
Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not happy with a service, please contact our support team immediately. We'll work with you and the provider to resolve the issue.

For Service Providers

How do I become a service provider on Lokal?
To become a provider, download the app and select "Become a Provider" during registration. You'll need to complete your profile, select the services you offer, set your availability and pricing, and verify your identity. Our team will review your application and get back to you within 2-3 business days.
Can I set my own prices and schedule?
Absolutely! As a provider, you have full control over your pricing and availability. Set your rates based on your experience and market research. You can also update your schedule anytime to mark when you're available to take bookings.
How does the verification process work?
For the safety of our community, all providers must complete identity verification. This includes submitting a valid ID, proof of address, and relevant certifications or licenses for specialized services. This helps build trust and ensures quality service for customers.

Technical Issues

The app is not loading or crashing. What should I do?
Try these troubleshooting steps: 1) Close and reopen the app, 2) Check your internet connection, 3) Make sure you have the latest version of the app, 4) Restart your device, 5) Clear the app cache in your device settings. If the problem persists, contact support with details about your device and the issue.
I'm not receiving notifications. How can I fix this?
Check your device settings to ensure notifications are enabled for Lokal. Go to your phone's Settings > Apps > Lokal > Notifications and make sure they're turned on. Also check that Do Not Disturb mode is off. If you still don't receive notifications, try logging out and back in.
Why can't I see service providers near me?
Make sure location permissions are enabled for the Lokal app in your device settings. The app needs access to your location to show nearby providers. If location is enabled and you still don't see providers, it might mean there are no active providers in your area at the moment.
